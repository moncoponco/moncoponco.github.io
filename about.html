ABOUT

this project is about shades, reflections, objects, hands, tags, pens, gesture, flickers, waves, and shines. The purpose of this project is to invite you to look at those small things that make up our every day and, with your eyes, find the patterns that surround us. -MONCOPONCO

(excerpt from 'rayuela' by julio cortázar)""""""""""""
