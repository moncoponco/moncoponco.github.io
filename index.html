double exposures
Contains window panes that act as a double exposure between the inside and the outside, juxtaposing retail spaces to trees, people and cars.
building reflections
Contains sun reflections from one building to its neighbors, presentings Xs and circles among other shapes. Its position changes to the time of the day, following the sun like a clock.
vessels
Contains a variety of drinking vessels with lights that reflect in and from its shape. Wine turns the light red, certain shapes cause specific dripping patterns, and the glass rivets concentrate the light in distinct patterns.
treasure hunts
Contains marking on the street that hint a playful treasure hunt sprawling through different metropolitan cities.
constructions
Contains consturction moments, where linear mounted structures and protective sheets glitter in front of illluminated windows and the sun, playing with a three-dimensional conjunction of lines and lights.
mirrors
Contains street mirrors made of different materials, playing with the idea of reflective feedback.

Contains asphalt markings of the letter M with a heart or a plus and another letter. The goal is to collect all of the alphabet combinations.
